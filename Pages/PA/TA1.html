CHEETAH
The cheetah is the fastest animal on Earth.
Belong to the cat family. Predator.
The cheetah has a red skin, covered with
black spots. The legs are long, thanks to
which the cheetah can run fast,
but not for long. A cheetah can live
6 days without food.
Black lines at the eyes help him to see
normally in strong sunshine. Because
of the structure of the spine, a cheetah
can keep its head level while running.
The tail, when running, acts as a stabilizer
and helps to control the body. The cheetah
is not large and at the withers is only
130cm, and the tail is 74cm long.
Cheetah kittens
are born from 1 to 5 individuals.
They are small, blind and helpless.
The cubs are covered with spots and short
yellow hair. On the back there is a
"patrimonial cape", it is a gray long coat
which passes as one grows older.
Herads love their offspring and therefore
take care of it.
Interesting facts about the cheetah:
(1) The cheetah can accelerate to 120 km / h.
(2) The cheetah is easy to draw and gets along well with a person.
(3) Unlike other feline cheetahs, the claws are always protruding.
